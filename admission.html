Enrollment Steps

Your guide to enrolling your child seamlessly.

Application Form

Begin the enrollment process by filling out our online form, which captures essential information about your child.

Interview

Our admissions team will schedule a meeting to better understand your child’s needs and answer questions.

Documentation

Provide necessary documents such as birth certificate, previous school records, and immunization forms.

Confirmation

You will receive an official confirmation of your child’s enrollment along with orientation details.
